About Correlations

Correlation (r) measures how strongly two things are related. Values range from -1 to +1:

  • Positive (+): When one goes up, the other tends to go up too
  • Negative (-): When one goes up, the other tends to go down
  • Near zero: No clear relationship

p-value indicates statistical significance. Values below 0.05 are considered significant (marked with ✓).
